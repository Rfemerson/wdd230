About Us

Our History

Bountiful Foods - Fresh Drinks is a company founded in 2005 in Carlsbad, California. Since then, we have been dedicated to creating fresh and healthy beverages with natural ingredients. We started with a local fruit stand, and with the support of the community, we expanded to our first store in Carlsbad. Our passion for excellence inspires us to constantly improve our recipes. Join us in our quest for a more refreshing life, one drink at a time! Thank you for your continued support!
