Potential investors and companies in the tourism sector looking for opportunities to expand their operations or invest in new ventures. It also seeks to reach tourists in search of tropical places, suitable for practicing sports such as kite-surfing.

Persona 2 - Peter Jordan

  • Age: 42
  • Ocupation: Business man
  • Location: Provo - USA

Backgroud

Peter is a successful investor who is always looking for new opportunities, he seeks to invest in the tourism industry and for that he looks for unexplored places but with high profitability potential.

  • Find places little explored by the tourism industry
  • Find high-yield opportunities
  • Join local businesses

Cons Scenarios

  • Little structure and knowledge of the natives for big business
  • Political instability of the country
  • Difficulty finding companies that undergo due diligence
